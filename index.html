HR Attrition Analytics Dashboard with Power BI

In this project, I made use of Power BI to analyze attrition inside an organization. The project's goal was to learn about attrition trends based on characteristics such as age group, education field, work satisfaction rate, department, and degree qualification. The project aimed to efficiently visualize and analyze data, detect attrition trends, and deliver actionable recommendations to manage turnover and improve employee retention tactics by leveraging the capabilities of Power BI. The project's findings can help HR professionals and organizational leaders better understand attrition patterns and implement data-driven steps to increase employee retention. Organizations may discover areas of concern, adopt focused solutions, and create a pleasant work environment that improves job satisfaction and minimizes attrition by utilizing Power BI's interactive visualizations and analytics capabilities. These insights and recommendations enable organizations to address attrition issues proactively, increase employee engagement, and achieve long-term success in talent retention.

Road Accident Analysis using power bi

This project focuses on road accident analysis using Power BI, the goal of the project was to learn more about casualties by vehicle type, monthly trends, casualties by area and location, casualty classifications (fatal, serious, minor), and the impact of weather and road surface conditions. The initiative aims to find patterns and contribute to the development of effective road safety measures by investigating these elements. The findings are useful for legislators, traffic officials, and other stakeholders involved in road safety measures. Understanding the patterns and risk variables discovered in this analysis allows for the development of targeted interventions, the improvement of infrastructure, and the implementation of preventive measures to reduce the frequency and severity of road accidents.

Analyzing the Customer Service of a restaurant using SQL (Microsoft SQL Server Management Studio)

This project focused on analyzing the customer service of a restaurant with the use of SQL, primarily Microsoft SQL Server Management Studio, to analyze a dataset that included client visit patterns, expenses, and favorite menu items. The goal was to better understand clients, provide a personalized experience, increase customer loyalty, and get important insights using SQL-based analysis. Through SQL, the restaurant can give a personalised experience, optimise menu offers, and increase customer loyalty by analyzing consumer visiting habits, spending, and favourite menu items. SQL also supplied insights through its querying capabilities, statistical functions, and data manipulation techniques, assisting in data exploration and supporting strategic decision-making. Overall, this project demonstrated the value of SQL in customer service analysis, allowing restaurants to better understand their consumers and promote better customer experiences and loyalty.

Bike Sales Analysis with Pivot Tables and Dashboards on Microsoft Excel

This project examines bike sales data. The research examined customer commuting, average income per purchase, customer age ranges, and whether customers still own automobiles using Microsoft Excel and its Pivot Table function. The project aims to acquire insights on bike sales trends and customer attributes by utilising Pivot tables and dashboards in Excel, enabling data-driven decision-making for marketing and sales initiatives. The project offered data exploration and visualization, supporting data-driven decision-making for marketing and sales initiatives by utilizing Excel's Pivot Table function and building dashboards. The results helped to improve pricing strategies, target particular customer groups, and investigate new market prospects by offering useful insights into client attributes, preferences, and possible market segments. Excel's pivot tables and dashboards have proven to be useful tools for analyzing bike sales, enabling companies to take well-informed decisions and succeed in the bike sales industry. .
